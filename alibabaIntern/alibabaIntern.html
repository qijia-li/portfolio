Introduction

Short videos have played a significant role in B2C platforms to help sellers sell their products on e-commerce websites like Taobao, Amazon and etc. 1688, one of the largest B2B ecommerce website, is exploring how can short videos help to increase sales volumn.


I worked on

Redefined the content of short video script for Business Buyers

I worked with the user research group and analyzed data about the performance of short video on the 1688 platform to figure out what are helpful strategies and contents of short videos for business buyers, The deliverable will be a website (expected to be launched in late April, 2020) contains all the conclusions and scripts for different product departments.

Cooperated with ISV to created 100 benchmarking videos

ISV is a department help sellers on the 1688 platform make short videos for their platform. Cooperation is the key to success in the business world. Thus, in order to check our solutions, we cooperated with ISV by offering them the scripts they want and they made 100 benchmarking videos for us as reference videos in the future. The benchmarking videos show great data performance on the platform, achieving an average 10% increment of effective video views.

Designed animating stickers and thems for product short videos for Lunar New Year Promotion.

We have the lunar new year promotion which is a good chance to encourage sellers use our strategies to make their product videos to gain more data about performance. I designed 4 themes and 16 stickers for product shor videos and here is part of them.

Takeaways

There were a number of important lessons I took away from this internship. Some of them I already find myself considering and integrating into my design practice, two things that stands out most are the value of data and research.

Data is always important and guide our design. During my experience back in the college, we were not able to gather enough data to help us make decision. Instead, we used user interview to get qualitative data to complement our user research. We tended to make assumption based on our previous experience or others' advices. Since there was no business goal or data tracking for our work, as students, we were not able to learn much from our design.

I also found how important it is to have real users state their needs and test the design.Business users is very different from the customers. Designers need to adjust a lot based on their needs
