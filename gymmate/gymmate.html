Gymmate

ROLE TIME SCOPE OF WORK
UX Designer Intern Apr.2020 to Sep.2021 UX Design, UX Research

Research

I conducted guerilla research in 10 gyms in Wuhan, trying to find out the shortcomings in the fitness area.After the research, we found that one problem occurs for both the stakeholder and the users is that the members find it hard to insist to work out.


PROBLEM STATEMENT

According to our guerilla research, we found that members in the gym have the needs of developing friends who can work out with them. Back up by scientific research, it is shown that people who have gymmates are easier to insist on working out in the gym. Thus, we decide to create our product——'GYMMATE', aiming to help people engage with the gym community and find gymmates who can work out with them..

“When we studied the drop-out rate of a year-long fitness program, we found that couples who entered the program together had only an eight percent drop-out rate, compared with roughly 50 percent of the people who entered the program alone. And we’ve found that you get similar results whether you’re working with a spouse, a friend, or within a group.”


——John Raglin, PhD, Professor of Kinesiology at Indiana University
